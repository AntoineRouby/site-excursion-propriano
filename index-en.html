The Secret of Our Adventures: The Narwhal Orca 1200

Our investment in this professional semi-rigid guarantees high cruising speed and maximum comfort, essential to cover long distances without fatigue and with privileged access to coastal areas.

  • Performance & Speed: A powerful engine to reach remote destinations quickly.
  • Coastal Exploration: The semi-rigid design allows us to skirt every cove and wild coast inaccessible to larger boats.
  • Premium Comfort: Suspended and ergonomic seats for 12 passengers, minimizing wave impact.
  • Small Group (Max 12 PAX): For an intimate experience and easy access to caves.

Article 4. Booking and Cancellation

4.2. Cancellation by the Customer

Any cancellation request must be sent in writing (email). Refunds will be made according to the following schedule, calculated from the scheduled departure date of the excursion:

  • Cancellation ≥ 7 days before departure: Full refund of all amounts paid.
  • Cancellation between 7 days and 48 hours before departure: Deposit retained or 30% of the total amount.
  • Cancellation < 48 hours before departure: No refund is possible.

4.3. Cancellation by Aria Maris (Weather or Safety)

Aria Maris is the sole judge of weather conditions. Safety and comfort are priorities.

Cancellation Decision: The captain may cancel up to departure time if weather conditions do not allow the excursion to be carried out under optimal safety or comfort conditions.

If Aria Maris cancels for weather or safety reasons, the customer will be offered:

  • Rescheduling to a later available date.
  • A full refund of all amounts paid.
