The Wonders of Scandola & Piana

Be charmed by the **Scandola Nature Reserve (UNESCO)**. Sail at the foot of the **volcanic red rock** cliffs of the Calanques de Piana, where time seems suspended. This is the refuge of **peregrine falcons** and rare endemic flora. Possible sightings of **dolphins** in these pure waters. (Full Day)

€95 / person (Drinks included)

The Limestone Dream of Bonifacio

Head south, where the **white chalk cliffs** of Bonifacio rise up, sculpted by legends and the wind. We explore secret sea caves and the **Lavezzi Islands**, a true natural aquarium with turquoise waters. These coves are a marvel for observing **Posidonia seagrass** and escaping. (Full Day)

€85 / person (Stops possible)

Dolphin Dance & Sunset Tour

A magical escape at the end of the day, when the sun sets the Gulf of Valinco ablaze. We actively search for **dolphins (Bottlenose Dolphins)**, the majestic hosts of our coasts. Experience this rare moment when marine life awakens at dusk. Ideal for families and nature lovers. (Duration 3h)

€70 / person (Guaranteed sighting)
